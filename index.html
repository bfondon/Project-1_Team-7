TourStation

Go Where Your Fans Are

Don't like playing in empty or dead rooms? Using Google Trends, find markets where you are frequently searched with the ability to drill down by Country, State, and City.

Choose Your Venue

Using FourSquare's API, pull top venues for your popular markets. So long, endless internet searches!

Build Your Set

Use Spotify driven data to determine your most popular songs in a given market. Put on a great show, grow your fanbase.

So, whats it all about?

We wanted to create a centralized location to help working bands locate venues for booking tours. Even more than that, we wanted to give musicians access to powerful databases that allow them to find markets where they are frequently searched or streamed while providing matching venue details - giving artists the best chance of performing in front of larger crowds and growing their fanbase.
